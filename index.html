STRATOCASTER®
Our popular Deep “C” neck now sports smooth rolled fingerboard edges,
a “Super-Natural” satin finish and a newly sculpted neck heel for a supremely
comfortable feel and easy access to the upper register.
New V-Mod II Stratocaster single-coil pickups are more
articulate than ever while retaining bell-like chime and warmth.
An upgraded 2-point tremolo with a cold-rolled steel block increases sustain,
clarity and high-end sparkle.

TELECASTER®
Bold, innovative and rugged, the Player Telecaster is pure Fender,
through and through. The feel, the style and,
most importantly, the sound—they’re all there,
waiting for you
to make them whisper or wail for your music.

JAGUAR®
Never resting in our mission to make life better for musicians,
we created the short-scale Jaguar guitar in 1962.
Originally released as our feature-rich,
top-of-the-line electric guitar, it inspired
the heroes of the musical underground and hit its stride with surf,
punk and alternative guitarists.
